About Us


Mike Wheeler's Dock Builders is your trusted builder of quality boat docks, walkways, decks, duck and deer blinds, and many more. Quality blinds for that outdoors man that is looking for more than just one hunting, fishing, and boating experience.


Nestled in the heart of Arkansas, Dock Builders operates with the belief that quality work and integrity is the key to our success. Since 1992 we have provided our valued customers with single stalls to multi-stall boat docks, walkways, swim and fishing platforms. In addition to providing your boat dock needs we also specialize in the manufacturing of deer blinds and duck blinds.
